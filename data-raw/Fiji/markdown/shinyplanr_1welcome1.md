This interactive visualization tool supports marine spatial planning (MSP) in Fiji through the [Blue Prosperity Fiji (BPF)](https://www.blueprosperityfiji.org) program — a government-led initiative to ensure the sustainable use of marine resources. 

Blue Prosperity Fiji is a partnership between the Government of Fiji and the Waitt Institute to support Fiji’s vision for a healthy ocean, thriving communities, and prosperous economies. The program focuses on sustainable ocean management of 100% of Fiji’s ocean and at least 30% full protection of Fiji’s waters in marine protected areas, in addition to sustainable fisheries management and development of Fiji’s ocean industries through Blue Economy initiatives. The program will incorporate community engagement, scientific research, and traditional knowledge to improve long-term economic stability, livelihoods, and ocean ecosystems. 

The scenarios used in this tool are informed by the goals and objectives that the MSP Working Group has developed. They draw on the latest science and data, including the recently completed Ocean Use Survey that maps human activities and datasets on marine habitats and species.

By using this tool, you are contributing to a transparent, evidence-based process where stakeholders can work together to shape the marine future of Fiji.
