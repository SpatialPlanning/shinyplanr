
What are ecosystem services?
-------

Ecosystem services are defined as the benefits that nature provides to human beings. They can be divided into:

* Provisioning services: tangible products provided by ecosystems (e.g. food, fresh water, etc.)
* Regulating services: benefits provided by the regulation of natural processes (e.g. climate regulation, water purification, pollination, etc.)
* Cultural services: non-material benefits derived by the direct connection of humans with ecosystems (e.g. recreation, cultural values, etc.)
* Supporting services: fundamental processes necessary for all other ecosystems to function (e.g. nutrient cycling, soil formation, oxygen production, etc.)
