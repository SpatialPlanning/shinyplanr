This interactive visualization tool supports marine spatial planning (MSP) in the Federated States of Micronesia (FSM) through the [Blue Prosperity Micronesia (BPM)](https://www.blueprosperitymicronesia.org) program — a government-led initiative to ensure the sustainable use of marine resources. In Kosrae, the tool contributes directly to developing its Marine Spatial Plan, as outlined in the Memorandum of Understanding signed by the Governor.

BPM takes a holistic approach, combining the best available science with active stakeholder participation, to create a healthier ocean that supports both people and future generations. This tool is one way to put that approach into practice. It allows you to explore the waters of Kosrae (out to the 12-nautical-mile State/National boundary), compare planning scenarios, and discuss options for balancing different ocean uses.

The scenarios used in this tool are informed by the goals and objectives that the MSP Working Group has developed. They draw on the latest science and data, including the recently completed Ocean Use Survey that maps human activities and datasets on marine habitats and species.

By using this tool, you are contributing to a transparent, evidence-based process where stakeholders can work together to shape the marine future of Kosrae.
